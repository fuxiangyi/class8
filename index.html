About this data visualization: It shows companies and investors information before 2013. In this visualization, it only shows out the top 100 companies, which receives more investments than others. Color code represents the information about companies categories: Ecommerce, Cleantech, Biotech, Web, Software, Mobile and many Others . The size of a circle represent the total amount of investments the company got before 2013, "Clearwire" got the most investments according to the dataset report.

As to the interact function, when user hover over on a selected circle, more information relate to the selected company will show up, and every time user clicked on the circle, companies’ investors information will present. The size of the circle represents the amount of investment from the investor to the selected company.
